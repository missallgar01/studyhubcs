3. Describe

[2]

Describe one way a local area network (LAN) is different from a wide area network (WAN).

Answer: A description such as:• A LAN covers a small (geographic) area / a WAN covers a large (geographic) area (2)• A LAN uses infrastructure owned by one company / a WAN uses third-party infrastructure (2)• A LAN connects individual devices / uses switches (1), whereas a WAN connects LANs / uses gateways (1)
