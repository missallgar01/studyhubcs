FAST FOUR STARTER

1. State/Identify

[1]

Identify the term that means breaking a problem or solution down into smaller parts.

Answer: Decomposition

2. Explain

[2]

Explain the best choice of secondary storage for the battery-operated mower.

Answer: The mower should use solid-state secondary storage (1) because it has no moving parts / can use very little power from the battery / is robust to bumps and knocks (1)

3. Describe

[2]

Describe one effect of using lossy compression to reduce the size of a file.

Answer: Award up to two marks for a linked description, such as:• (Removed) data (1) cannot be restored / means that the original file/information cannot be fully recreated/represented (1)• When data is removed (1) humans may perceive/interpret the image/sound/text/information differently (1)

4. Construct

[4]

Construct an expression to calculate the transmission rate, in megabits per second, required to transmit a 1.4 gibibyte file in 13 minutes. You do not need to do the calculation.

Answer: Award one mark for:• 13 × 60 or 780 (seconds) (1)• 1.4 × 8 × 1024³ (1)• 1000² (1)• Fully correct response (1)Example:  (1.4 × 8 × 1024³)  ———————————                   (13 × 60 × 1000²)
