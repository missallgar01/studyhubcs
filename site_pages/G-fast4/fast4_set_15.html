3. Construct

Construct an expression to calculate the file size, in mebibytes, of a CD quality (44.1 KHz, bit depth of 16), two-channel stereo soundtrack that is 4 minutes long. You do not need to carry out the calculation.

Answer: One mark for each:• Sample rate and bit depth = 44.1 × 1000 × 16 (1)• Channels and time = 2 × 4 × 60 (1)• Unit conversions = 8 × 1024 × 1024 (1)• Numerator and denominator the right way around (1)Example of an expression that gains full marks:((44.1 × 1000 × 16) × (2 × 4 × 60)) / (8 × 1024 × 1024)
