1. Describe

[2]

Describe one disadvantage of using a bus topology.

Answer: • If the main cable fails / is damaged , the whole network will fail
• If more computers are connected , the transmission time is increased / increased chance of data collisions
• Every computer receives all of the data on the network , causing a security risk

2. Explain

[2]

Robots use sensors to collect data about their surroundings in order to carry out actions independently. Explain one way that a modern car is a robot.

Answer: • The wipers/lights come on automatically because sensors detect rain/darkness
• Speed is automatically adjusted / brakes are applied automatically / alarm sounded because sensors detect distance from other cars / obstacles / people
• The temperature is adjusted automatically because a sensor/thermometer monitors the change in temperature of the passenger compartment / car interior

3. Give

[2]

Give two reasons for using a star topology rather than a bus topology.

Answer: • A star is more scalable than a bus because you can add extra devices without disrupting the network
• A star is more fault tolerant/reliable because a damaged cable/faulty device can be replaced/removed without disrupting the rest of the network
• It is easier to troubleshoot/isolate faults because all cables are attached to a central point
• A star is capable of handling higher volumes of network traffic than a bus
