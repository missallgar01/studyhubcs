4. Describe

Describe the process of converting a binary number to two's complement.

Answer: A linked description such as: • Flip all the bits (1) and add one (1) • Copy/keep all the 0s from the right/LSB, up to and including the first 1 (1), then flip the remaining bits (1) • Flip all the bits from the left/MSB (1) up to, but not including, the 1 nearest the right/LSB (1)
