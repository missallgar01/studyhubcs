FAST FOUR STARTER

1. Name

[1]

Name the characteristic of a wireless network that is measured in metres.

Answer: Range

2. Describe

[2]

Describe one way a local area network (LAN) is different from a wide area network (WAN).

Answer: • A description such as:
• A LAN covers a small (geographic) area / a WAN covers a large (geographic) area (2)
• A LAN uses infrastructure owned by one company / a WAN uses third-party infrastructure (2)
• A LAN connects individual devices / uses switches , whereas a WAN connects LANs / uses gateways

3. State/Identify

[2]

State one way users could reduce the environmental impact of digital technology.

Answer: • Reduce use
• Responsible disposal (to prevent leaking chemicals)
• Recycling
• Reuse
• Replace devices less frequently
• Use power-saving options (such as black screen)

4. Describe

[2]

A replacement cycle is the time between the purchase of a device and the purchase of its replacement. Describe one impact the length of replacement cycles has on the environment.

Answer: • Shorter replacement cycles lead to devices being disposed of more often , which leads to more e-waste/landfill/pollution
• Shorter replacement cycles lead to more manufacturing , which incurs increased extraction of metals/increased use of water to manufacture chips/use of fossil fuels
• Long replacement cycles mean devices are not disposed of , which reduces the amount of e-waste/landfill/emissions
• Long replacement cycles mean fewer devices are manufactured , which reduces the need for extraction of metals/the need for water to manufacture chips/less energy required
